- Outer radius (\( R \)): 6 cm

The volume of the pipe is the difference between the volume of the outer cylinder and the volume of the inner cylinder. The formula for the volume of a cylinder is:
\[
V = \pi r^2 h
\]

\[
V_{\text{outer}} = \pi R^2 h = \pi (6)^2 (13)

\[
V_{\text{inner}} = \pi r^2 h = \pi (4)^2 (13)

\[
V_{\text{inner}} = \pi (16)(13) = 208\pi \text{ cm}^3
\]

#### Step 3: Calculate the volume of the pipe
The volume of the pipe is the difference between the volume of the outer cylinder and the volume of the inner cylinder:
\[
V_{\text{pipe}} = V_{\text{outer}} - V_{\text{inner}}
\]
\[
V_{\text{pipe}} = 468\pi - 208\pi = 260\pi \text{ cm}^3
\]

\[
V_{\text{pipe}} = 260\pi \approx 260 \times 3.14159 = 816.8134 \text{ cm}^3
