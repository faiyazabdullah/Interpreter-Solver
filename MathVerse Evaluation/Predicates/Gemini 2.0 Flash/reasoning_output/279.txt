Question: In quadrilateral R Q T S, \angle R = \angle T = x degrees. Find m \angle R.
================================================================================

- The image shows a quadrilateral R Q T S.
- Points: R, Q, T, S
- Angles: Angle R = x, Angle Q = (2x + 5), Angle T = x, Angle S = (2x + 7)
- The sum of the interior angles of a quadrilateral is 360 degrees.

STEP 2: CONSTRAINT AND RELATIONSHIP SYNTHESIS
- Angle R + Angle Q + Angle T + Angle S = 360
- x + (2x + 5) + x + (2x + 7) = 360

STEP 3: QUESTION-DRIVEN COMPLETENESS CHECK
- The information is sufficient to solve the problem by setting up an equation.
