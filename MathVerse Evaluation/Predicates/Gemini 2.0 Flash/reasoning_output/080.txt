STEP 3: ANGLE AND PERPENDICULARITY ANALYSIS

STEP 4: CONGRUENCE AND EQUALITY ANALYSIS

STEP 5: INTERSECTION AND POSITIONING ANALYSIS
Point A lies on the top circle, and point B lies on the bottom circle.

We can unfold the cylinder into a rectangle. The height of the rectangle is 5, and the width is 24. The shortest distance is the straight line distance between A and B. We can use the Pythagorean theorem.
