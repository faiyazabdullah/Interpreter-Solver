Question: The radius of the figure is $ 7 \mathrm{~cm}$. Find the perimeter of the sector shown, correct to two decimal places.

The image shows a sector of a circle.
The sector has a radius of 7 cm and a central angle of 40 degrees.
Let O be the center of the circle, and A and B be the endpoints of the radii.
The sector is OAB.

STEP 2: CIRCLE-SPECIFIC ANALYSIS
The sector is part of a circle with center O.
OA and OB are radii, so LengthOf(OA) = LengthOf(OB) = 7 cm.
Arc(A,B) is the arc of the sector.

STEP 3: ANGLE AND PERPENDICULARITY ANALYSIS
Angle(A,O,B) is the central angle of the sector, and its measure is 40 degrees.

STEP 4: CONGRUENCE AND EQUALITY ANALYSIS
LengthOf(OA) = LengthOf(OB)

STEP 5: INTERSECTION AND POSITIONING ANALYSIS
Points A and B lie on the circle.

STEP 6: CONSTRAINT AND RELATIONSHIP SYNTHESIS
The perimeter of the sector is the sum of the lengths of the two radii and the arc length.
Arc length = (central angle / 360) * 2 * pi * radius
Arc length = (40/360) * 2 * pi * 7
Arc length = (1/9) * 14 * pi
