Question: The diagonals of rhombus F G H J intersect at K. If G H = x + 9 and J H = 5 x - 2, find x.
================================================================================

- The figure shows a rhombus FGHJ.

STEP 2: CIRCLE-SPECIFIC ANALYSIS

STEP 3: ANGLE AND PERPENDICULARITY ANALYSIS
- The diagonals of a rhombus are perpendicular. Therefore, angle FKJ, FKH, HKG, GKF, JKG, JKH are right angles.

STEP 4: CONGRUENCE AND EQUALITY ANALYSIS
- All sides of a rhombus are equal. Therefore, FG = GH = HJ = JF.

STEP 5: INTERSECTION AND POSITIONING ANALYSIS
- Diagonals intersect at point K.

STEP 6: CONSTRAINT AND RELATIONSHIP SYNTHESIS
- Since FGHJ is a rhombus, GH = JH. This gives us the equation x+9 = 5x-2.
