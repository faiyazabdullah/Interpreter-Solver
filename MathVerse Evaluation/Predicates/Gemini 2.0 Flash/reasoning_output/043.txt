Question: As shown in the figure, triangle ODC is the figure obtained by rotating triangle OAB clockwise around point O by 30.0. If point D happens to fall on AB, and the degree of angle AOC is 100.0, then the degree of angle DOB is ()
================================================================================

STEP 1: COMPREHENSIVE IMAGE ANALYSIS
- We see two triangles, OAB and ODC.
- Point O is a vertex for both triangles.
- The diagram shows that triangle OAB is rotated around point O to produce triangle ODC.
- The rotation angle is 30 degrees (angle AOD).
- Angle AOC measures 100 degrees.
- Point D lies on line AB.

STEP 2: CIRCLE-SPECIFIC ANALYSIS
- Although a circle is not explicitly drawn, the rotation implies that points A, B, C, and D lie on a circle centered at O if we assume OA=OC and OB=OD.

STEP 3: ANGLE AND PERPENDICULARITY ANALYSIS
- Angle AOD = 30 degrees
- Angle AOC = 100 degrees
- Angle DOC = Angle AOB (due to rotation)
- Angle AOB can be expressed as AOD + DOB
- Angle BOC can be expressed as AOC - AOB
- Angle DOB is what we want to find.
- Since the rotation is 30 degrees, angle AOD = angle BOC = 30 degrees.

STEP 4: CONGRUENCE AND EQUALITY ANALYSIS
- Triangle OAB is congruent to triangle ODC.
- OA = OD, OB = OC, and AB = DC.
- Angle OAB = angle ODC.
- Angle OBA = angle OCD.
- Angle AOB = angle DOC.

STEP 5: INTERSECTION AND POSITIONING ANALYSIS
- Point D lies on line AB.
- Lines OA, OB, OC, and OD intersect at point O.

STEP 6: CONSTRAINT AND RELATIONSHIP SYNTHESIS
- Angle AOC = 100, AOD = 30. Therefore, Angle DOC = AOC - AOD = 100 - 30 = 70
- Angle DOC = AOB. Therefore, Angle AOB = 70.
- Angle AOB = AOD + DOB. 70 = 30 + DOB. Therefore, DOB = 40.
- BOC = 30
- COD + DOB + BOA = 360. Also COD = AOB

STEP 7: QUESTION-DRIVEN COMPLETENESS CHECK
- The key relationships identified are the congruence between the triangles, the rotation angle, and the given angle AOC.
