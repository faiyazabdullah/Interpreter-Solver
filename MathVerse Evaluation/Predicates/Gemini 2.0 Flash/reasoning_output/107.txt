STEP 6: CONSTRAINT AND RELATIONSHIP SYNTHESIS
- Since AB is a diameter, angle ADB is a right angle.
- Inscribed angle theorem: The measure of an inscribed angle is half the measure of its intercepted arc.
- Angle AOD + Angle BOD = 180 degrees since AB is a straight line. Therefore, Angle AOD = 180 - 130 = 50 degrees.
- Angle ACD = Angle ABD since they subtend the same arc.
- Angle AOD = 2*Angle ABD.  So, 50 = 2*Angle ABD.  Therefore, Angle ABD = 25 degrees.
- Therefore, Angle ACD = 25 degrees.
