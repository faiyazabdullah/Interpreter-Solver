- Center: O

- Radii: OA, OB
- Chord: AB
- Inscribed Angle: ACB

STEP 3: ANGLE AND PERPENDICULARITY ANALYSIS
- Angle ACB = 25 degrees
- Triangle OAB is isosceles because OA = OB (radii)
- Angle OAB = Angle OBA

STEP 4: CONGRUENCE AND EQUALITY ANALYSIS
- OA = OB (radii of the same circle)
- Triangle OAB is isosceles

STEP 5: INTERSECTION AND POSITIONING ANALYSIS
- Points A, B, and C lie on circle O.
- Lines OA and OB intersect at point O.

STEP 6: CONSTRAINT AND RELATIONSHIP SYNTHESIS
- Angle AOB = 2 * Angle ACB (central angle is twice the inscribed angle)
- Since triangle OAB is isosceles, angles OAB and OBA are equal. The sum of the angles in triangle OAB is 180 degrees.
