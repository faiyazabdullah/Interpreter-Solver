STEP 2: CIRCLE-SPECIFIC ANALYSIS

- Angle JRK = Angle KRL = Angle LRM = Angle MRN = Angle NRP = Angle PRJ

STEP 5: INTERSECTION AND POSITIONING ANALYSIS

STEP 6: CONSTRAINT AND RELATIONSHIP SYNTHESIS
- Since the hexagon is regular, it can be divided into 6 congruent equilateral triangles.
- Each central angle is 360/6 = 60 degrees.
- Triangle RKL is an isosceles triangle since RK = RL.
- Since RS is perpendicular to KL, RS bisects KL and angle KRL.
