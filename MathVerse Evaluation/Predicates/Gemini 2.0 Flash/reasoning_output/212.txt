- The angles $(8y+2)^\circ$ and $(25y-20)^\circ$ are corresponding angles since the rays they lie on are marked parallel and these angles lie on the same side of the intersecting line. Therefore they are equal.

STEP 4: CONGRUENCE AND EQUALITY ANALYSIS
- The angle $10x^\circ$ is equal to $(8y+2)^\circ$ because they are vertical angles.
- The angle $(8y+2)^\circ$ is equal to $(25y-20)^\circ$ because they are corresponding angles formed by parallel lines.

STEP 5: INTERSECTION AND POSITIONING ANALYSIS

    $10x = 8y + 2$ and $8y + 2 = 25y - 20$. From these equations we can solve for both $x$ and $y$.
    Since the problem only asks to find $y$, the first equation is not required.
