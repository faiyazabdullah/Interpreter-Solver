Question: Find the volume of the cone shown. The slant height is 8 centimeters and radius is 2 centimeters. 

Round your answer to two decimal places.
================================================================================

STEP 1: COMPREHENSIVE IMAGE ANALYSIS
- The image shows a cone.
- The slant height of the cone is 8 cm.
- The radius of the base of the cone is 2 cm.
- I need to calculate the volume of the cone.
- To calculate the volume, I need the height of the cone.
- I can find the height using the Pythagorean theorem with the slant height and the radius.

STEP 2: PYTHAGOREAN THEOREM
- height^2 + radius^2 = slant height^2
- height^2 + 2^2 = 8^2
- height^2 + 4 = 64
- height^2 = 60
- height = sqrt(60)

STEP 3: VOLUME CALCULATION
- Volume of a cone = (1/3) * pi * radius^2 * height
- Volume = (1/3) * pi * 2^2 * sqrt(60)
- Volume = (1/3) * pi * 4 * sqrt(60)
- Volume = (4/3) * pi * sqrt(60)
