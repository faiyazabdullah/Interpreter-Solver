STEP 2: CIRCLE-SPECIFIC ANALYSIS
- Circle center: O
- Points on circle: A, B, C
- Radii: OA, OB
- Chord: AB, BC
- Inscribed angle: ACB
- Central angle: AOB

STEP 3: ANGLE AND PERPENDICULARITY ANALYSIS
- Angle C = 35 degrees is an inscribed angle
- Angle AOB is a central angle subtended by the same arc AB as angle C

STEP 4: CONGRUENCE AND EQUALITY ANALYSIS
- OA = OB (both are radii of the same circle)
- Triangle OAB is an isosceles triangle

STEP 5: INTERSECTION AND POSITIONING ANALYSIS
- Points A, B, C lie on the circle.
- O is the center of the circle.

STEP 6: CONSTRAINT AND RELATIONSHIP SYNTHESIS
- The central angle AOB is twice the inscribed angle ACB subtending the same arc.
- Angle AOB = 2 * Angle ACB
