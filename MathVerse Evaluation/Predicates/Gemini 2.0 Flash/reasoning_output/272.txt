- There is a right angle in the triangle.

STEP 4: CONGRUENCE AND EQUALITY ANALYSIS
- Since the triangle is a 45-45-90 triangle, the two legs are equal.

- The triangle is a 45-45-90 triangle, so the sides are in the ratio 1:1:sqrt(2).
- If one leg is 4, the other leg is also 4. Then x = 4 * sqrt(2).
