Question: The figure shows a cylinder with height of 8 centimeters and radius of 6 centimeters. The volume of a cylinder is given by $V=\pi r^2h$. 

Find the volume of the cylinder, rounding your answer to two decimal places.
================================================================================

STEP 1: COMPREHENSIVE IMAGE ANALYSIS
- The image shows a cylinder.
- The cylinder has a radius of 6 cm and a height of 8 cm.
- The volume of a cylinder is given by $V = \pi r^2 h$.

STEP 2: CYLINDER-SPECIFIC ANALYSIS
- The cylinder has a circular base.
- The radius of the circular base is 6 cm.
- The height of the cylinder is 8 cm.

STEP 3: VOLUME CALCULATION
- The volume of the cylinder is $V = \pi * 6^2 * 8 = \pi * 36 * 8 = 288\pi$.
- $288\pi \approx 904.77868423$.
- Rounding to two decimal places, the volume is approximately 904.78 cubic centimeters.
