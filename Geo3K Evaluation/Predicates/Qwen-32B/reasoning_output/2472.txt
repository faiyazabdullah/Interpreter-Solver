QUESTION: Triangle L M N is equilateral, and M P bisects L N. Find the measure of the side of \triangle L M N.

REASONING:
================================================================================
### STEP-BY-STEP REASONING

#### STEP 1: IMAGE ANALYSIS
- **Geometric Shapes and Points:**
  - Triangle \( \triangle LMN \) with vertices \( L, M, N \).
  - Point \( P \) lies on line segment \( LN \).
  - Line segment \( MP \) connects vertex \( M \) to point \( P \).

- **Lines and Segments:**
  - \( LM \) is one side of the triangle with length \( 3x + 1 \).
  - \( MN \) is another side of the triangle with length \( 4x - 2 \).
  - \( LN \) is the base of the triangle, divided into two segments by point \( P \): \( LP \) and \( PN \).

- **Angles:**
  - \( \angle LMP = 5y^\circ \) is marked at point \( M \).

- **Special Markings:**
  - \( MP \) is labeled as the median (it bisects \( LN \)).
  - No right angles or other specific angle measures are marked.

#### STEP 2: RELATIONSHIP IDENTIFICATION
- **Triangle Properties:**
  - \( \triangle LMN \) is equilateral, meaning all sides are equal in length: \( LM = MN = LN \).
  - All internal angles of an equilateral triangle are \( 60^\circ \).

- **Median Properties:**
  - \( MP \) is the median of \( \triangle LMN \), dividing \( LN \) into two equal segments: \( LP = PN \).

- **Congruence:**
  - Since \( \triangle LMN \) is equilateral, all sides are congruent, and all medians are also equal.

#### STEP 3: MEASUREMENT ANALYSIS
- **Given Measurements:**
  - Side \( LM = 3x + 1 \).
  - Side \( MN = 4x - 2 \).
  - Since \( \triangle LMN \) is equilateral, \( LM = MN = LN \). Therefore:
    \[
    3x + 1 = 4x - 2
    \]

- **Angle Measurement:**
  - \( \angle LMP = 5y^\circ \). Since \( \triangle LMN \) is equilateral, \( \angle LMP \) is part of the internal structure but does not directly affect the side lengths.

#### STEP 4: QUESTION ANALYSIS
- The question asks for the measure of the side of \( \triangle LMN \).
- To find the side length, we need to solve for \( x \) using the equality \( 3x + 1 = 4x - 2 \).
- Once \( x \) is found, substitute it back into either \( 3x + 1 \) or \( 4x - 2 \) to get the side length.

#### STEP 5: PREDICATE PLANNING
- Represent the equilateral triangle and its properties.
- Capture the relationship between the sides \( LM \) and \( MN \).
- Indicate that \( MP \) is the median and bisects \( LN \).
- Use the given measurements and relationships without directly solving for the final answer.
